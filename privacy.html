Privacy Policy for Ruthless

Last updated: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Ruthless ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our task management application.

2. Information We Collect

Ruthless stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Stored Locally:

  • Your tasks and task completion history
  • App preferences and settings
  • Premium subscription status (via Google Play)
  • Notification preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:

  • Display your tasks and track your progress
  • Calculate your discipline score and statistics
  • Send local notifications (if enabled)
  • Manage your premium subscription status

4. Third-Party Services

Ruthless integrates with the following third-party services:

Google Play In-App Purchases

For premium subscriptions, we use Google Play's billing system. Google Play may collect payment information and purchase history according to their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Using device lock screen protection
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Being cautious about granting app permissions to other apps

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app from your device, or
  • Going to your device Settings → Apps → Ruthless → Storage → Clear Data

7. Children's Privacy

Ruthless does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: ketank1000@gmail.com

GitHub: https://github.com/ketank1000/ruthless

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store data on external servers, you have full control over your data. You can:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export or backup your data (feature coming soon)
